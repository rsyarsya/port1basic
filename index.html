Head Organizer of GAMASULTRA 2025

(2024—2025)

As the Head Organizer of GAMASULTRA 2025, I had the incredible opportunity to lead GAMASULTRA Goes to School, a program where students from Southeast Sulawesi introduced UGM to high schoolers across the region. This role challenged me to manage time effectively, coordinate students from diverse backgrounds, and build strong collaborations with schools to ensure a smooth execution of the event. Beyond logistics and planning, I gained invaluable leadership experience—learning how to navigate different perspectives, foster teamwork, and handle unexpected challenges. It was a rewarding journey that strengthened my ability to lead and speak in front of crowds with confidence and adaptability.

Vice Project Leader 2 IEEE Tech4Good DTETI X DTMK X TELKOM

(2025—now)

As Vice Project Leader 2 in the IEEE Tech4Good DTETI x DTMK x TELKOM initiative, I had the unique opportunity to be part of the Board of Directors for a critical infrastructure project—bringing clean water access to drought-affected communities in Dukuh Pengos, Gunung Kidul. Leading the Power Generation and Instrumental divisions, I navigated the challenge of managing a team that included senior students while maintaining professionalism and strong leadership. Being entrusted with this role as a first-year student was both an honor and a learning experience, pushing me to develop adaptability, strategic thinking, and effective communication. Through teamwork and dedication, my team exceeded expectations, ultimately gaining approval from our faculty advisors. This project reinforced my belief in using technology for meaningful social impact, and I'm incredibly proud of what we accomplished together.
